# Product Vision

## Project Name

**PharmaVision BI**

---

## Overview

PharmaVision BI is an interactive Business Intelligence platform designed to help pharmaceutical commercial teams monitor sales performance, identify business opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through intuitive dashboards and AI-assisted insights.

The project recreates a real-world enterprise analytics scenario inspired by the pharmaceutical industry using a fully synthetic dataset. This approach preserves confidentiality while maintaining realistic business complexity.

The platform combines data engineering, analytics, visualization, and Generative AI into a single end-to-end solution.

---

# Vision Statement

Empower commercial decision-makers with fast, reliable, and interactive analytics that transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.

---

# Business Problem

Commercial organizations frequently receive sales information from multiple distributors, regions, and sales channels.

As the amount of data grows, obtaining a unified view of business performance becomes increasingly difficult.

Without a centralized analytics solution, commercial teams spend significant time preparing reports instead of analyzing results and making strategic decisions.

---

# Challenges

- Limited visibility across commercial regions.
- Manual reporting processes.
- Difficulty identifying growth opportunities.
- Multiple hierarchical levels of analysis.
- Slow decision-making.

---

# Proposed Solution

Develop an interactive Business Intelligence platform capable of:

- Centralizing commercial sales information.
- Providing dynamic filtering across multiple business dimensions.
- Monitoring key business KPIs.
- Supporting hierarchical analysis.
- Generating AI-assisted business insights.

# Business Objectives

- Centralize commercial performance information.

- Improve sales visibility.

# Success Criteria

The product will be considered successful when users can:

- Understand commercial performance in minutes.
- Navigate through hierarchical sales information intuitively.
- Answer key business questions without manual calculations.
- Obtain AI-assisted explanations of business trends.
- Explore sales data interactively.
