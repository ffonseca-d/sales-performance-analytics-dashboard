# User Personas

## Overview

PharmaVision BI is designed to support multiple business stakeholders across different organizational levels.

Each persona interacts with the platform differently depending on their responsibilities, business goals, and decision-making needs.

---

# Persona 1 — Commercial Director

## Profile

The Commercial Director oversees the overall commercial performance of the organization and is responsible for defining strategic initiatives.

This user requires a high-level overview of business performance without needing to analyze operational details.

---

## Responsibilities

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Evaluate revenue growth.
- Compare regional performance.
- Track strategic KPIs.
- Identify business opportunities.

---

## Goals

- Increase revenue.
- Improve commercial efficiency.
- Detect market opportunities.
- Support strategic decision-making.

---

## Pain Points

- Multiple disconnected reports.
- Slow access to information.
- Limited visibility across regions.
- Time-consuming manual reporting.

---

## Dashboard Usage

The Commercial Director primarily interacts with:

- Executive Overview
- KPI Cards
- Revenue Trends
- Regional Comparison
- AI-generated Executive Summary

---

# Persona 2 — Regional Sales Manager

## Profile

Regional Sales Managers supervise commercial performance within their assigned territories.

Their primary focus is identifying underperforming districts, routes, and distributors.

---

## Responsibilities

- Monitor regional sales.
- Track distributor performance.
- Compare territories.
- Identify declining products.
- Improve regional execution.

---

## Goals

- Increase regional sales.
- Improve distributor productivity.
- Detect performance deviations early.
- Optimize sales strategies.

---

## Pain Points

- Difficulty identifying regional trends.
- Lack of detailed operational visibility.
- Manual analysis of multiple reports.

---

## Dashboard Usage

The Regional Manager frequently uses:

- Geographic Analysis
- Performance Dashboard
- Dynamic Filters
- Ranking Tables
- Drill-down Analysis

---

# Persona 3 — Business Analyst

## Profile

Business Analysts perform detailed analysis and provide insights that support commercial teams.

They require flexible exploration capabilities and access to detailed business metrics.

---

## Responsibilities

- Analyze commercial performance.
- Generate reports.
- Validate KPIs.
- Identify business trends.
- Support decision-making.

---

## Goals

- Produce reliable analyses.
- Identify business opportunities.
- Improve reporting quality.
- Reduce manual analysis time.

---

## Pain Points

- Large volumes of data.
- Repetitive manual reporting.
- Data quality inconsistencies.
- Limited self-service analytics.

---

## Dashboard Usage

Business Analysts interact with almost every section of the application, especially:

- Interactive Filters
- Time Series Analysis
- Product Performance
- Customer Analysis
- AI-assisted Insights

---

# Persona Journey

| Persona | Primary Goal | Main Dashboard |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Commercial Director | Strategic monitoring | Executive Overview |
| Regional Sales Manager | Territory optimization | Geographic Analysis |
| Business Analyst | Data exploration | Performance Analysis |

---

# Design Considerations

To address the needs of all personas, PharmaVision BI follows these design principles:

- Executive information first.
- Progressive drill-down analysis.
- Minimal cognitive load.
- Fast access to KPIs.
- Consistent navigation.
- Explainable AI insights.
- Interactive exploration without requiring technical knowledge.
