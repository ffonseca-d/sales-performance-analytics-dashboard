# Business Questions

## Overview

Business Intelligence is not about displaying data—it is about answering business questions that enable better decisions.

The purpose of PharmaVision BI is to help commercial teams quickly identify trends, opportunities, and risks through interactive analytics.

The following business questions define the functional scope of the platform and guide the design of every KPI and visualization.

---

# Executive Questions

These questions support strategic decision-making.

## EQ-01

**How much revenue was generated during the selected period?**

**Business Value**

Provides an immediate understanding of overall commercial performance.

---

## EQ-02

**How has revenue changed compared to the previous year?**

**Business Value**

Measures business growth and helps identify positive or negative trends.

---

## EQ-03

**Which regions contribute the most to total revenue?**

**Business Value**

Supports strategic investment and resource allocation decisions.

---

## EQ-04

**Which product categories drive the business?**

**Business Value**

Helps identify the strongest commercial segments.

---

## EQ-05

**Are we achieving our commercial objectives?**

**Business Value**

Provides an executive summary of overall business performance.

# Design Principles

Every KPI, chart, filter, and AI-generated explanation included in PharmaVision BI must answer at least one business question.

If a visualization does not support a business decision, it should not be included in the dashboard.

---

# Why this matters

Business Intelligence solutions should not be designed around charts—they should be designed around decisions.

By defining business questions before implementing the dashboard, the project ensures that every visualization has a clear purpose and delivers measurable business value.
